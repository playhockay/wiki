## How to pick a team

You do not pick a team. You open this page, you look at the names, the cities, the colors,
and something will feel correct. That feeling is not random. [Le Council](/lore/lpchuip)
cannot explain it further. Go with it.

## Rosters

15 players per team. Additional players exist as free agents. Each carries a public
[1–5 star rating](/guides/faq#what-are-star-ratings). Players evolve over time through
[modifiers](/modifiers/) gained from gameplay events and time in [the Sixth](/lore/the-sixth).

## Expansion

[The Almighty Ice](/lore/almighty-ice) appeared in twenty cities. If The Almighty Ice
decides to appear elsewhere, there will be more teams. [Le Council](/lore/lpchuip) does
not control this.
