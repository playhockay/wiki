title: Glossary

A living reference of Hockay terminology. Add entries by opening a
[pull request](https://github.com/playhockay/wiki)—the "Edit this page" link
below lands on GitHub's web editor for this file. Cross-link generously—most
terms have a dedicated page.

## A

- **Advisory**—see [LPCHUIP advisories](/modifiers/advisories).
- **The Almighty Ice**—the god of Hockay. Alive, intent-ful, spreading. [→ Page](/lore/almighty-ice)
- **Ancient**—ambiguous player modifier. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#ancient)
- **Arena modifier**—semi-permanent trait tied to a team's home building. [→ Page](/modifiers/arena)
- **Awake**—positive player modifier. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#awake)

## B

- **Bracket**—the single-elimination playoff structure. Pairings are random.
- **Brittle**—negative player modifier. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#brittle)

## C

- **Clean Ice**—a [match modifier](/modifiers/match#clean-ice) declaring no modifier. Announced as a modifier.

## D

- **Double or Nothing**—[match modifier](/modifiers/match#double-or-nothing). Double points, double payouts.
- **Doubled**—ambiguous player modifier. One event per game duplicates. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#doubled)

## E

- **Echo**—positive player modifier. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#echo)

## F

- **The Final**—the last playoff game. One-hour periods. [→ Page](/guides/season-cycle#day-3--the-final)
- **Flicker**—arena modifier at [The Watch Station](/teams/anchorage-auroras).
- **Fog**—[match modifier](/modifiers/match#fog). Goalies see less.
- **Foggy**—negative player modifier. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#foggy)

## G

- **Glacified**—the Hockay term for classified. [→ Page](/lore/glacified)

## H

- **Hollow**—weird player modifier. Effects [Glacified](/lore/glacified). [→ Page](/modifiers/player#hollow)
- **H.O.R.N.**—Hockay Official Relay Network. The broadcast arm of LPCHUIP. [→ Page](/lore/organizations#horn--hockay-official-relay-network)
- **Hockay**—the sport. Pronounced "Hockay" or "Hoqué." Not "Hockey."

## I

- **Isolation**—arena modifier at [The Last Terminal](/teams/vladivostok-vodkas). Suppresses all external modifiers on visitors.

## J

- **The Jambono**—resurfaces reality. Caretaker or corruptor. [→ Page](/lore/the-jambono)
- **JM**—Jean-Michel "JM" Laflèche. Play-by-play commentator. [→ Page](/lore/commentators)

## L

- **Looneys**—the official currency of Hockay. [→ Page](/guides/economy)
- **LPCHUIP**—Le Permanent Council du Hockay and Unexplained Ice Phenomena. The governing body. [→ Page](/lore/lpchuip)
- **Lure**—arena modifier at [The Still Strait](/teams/stockholm-sirens).
- **Luminous**—positive player modifier. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#luminous)

## M

- **Magnetic**—ambiguous player modifier. [The Puck](/lore/the-puck) finds them. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#magnetic)
- **Match modifier**—per-game condition announced in an advisory. [→ Page](/modifiers/match)
- **Matchday**—a full slate of 10 games. The regular season has 38.
- **Mercury**—[match modifier](/modifiers/match#mercury). Losing team gets cumulative boost.

## O

- **O.D.D.S.**—Office of Data-Driven Speculation. Publishes odds. [→ Page](/lore/organizations#odds--office-of-data-driven-speculation)
- **O.M.E.N.**—Oracle for Mathematical Estimation and Numerology. NOT affiliated. [→ Page](/lore/organizations#omen--oracle-for-mathematical-estimation-and-numerology)
- **Overclock**—arena modifier at [The Neon Crossing](/teams/tokyo-titans). More events per period.

## P

- **Period**—15 minutes in regular season, 60 minutes in [The Final](/guides/season-cycle#day-3--the-final).
- **Player modifier**—trait gained, lost, or swapped in [the Sixth](/lore/the-sixth). [→ Page](/modifiers/player)
- **The Puck**—the referee. Speaks once per season. [→ Page](/lore/the-puck)

## R

- **Rest Day**—the day after [The Final](/guides/season-cycle#day-3--the-final). No games. No announcements.
- **The Resurfacing**—what [the Jambono](/lore/the-jambono) does.
- **R.I.N.K.**—Royal Institute for Nascent Kinetics. Affiliated institution. Nature [Glacified](/lore/glacified). [→ Page](/lore/organizations#rink--the-royal-institute-for-nascent-kinetics)
- **Riviera**—arena modifier at [The Coastal Pavilion](/teams/rimini-rinklers). The only dual modifier.

## S

- **Sharp**—positive player modifier. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#sharp)
- **The Sixth**—the sixth dimension. Where the penalty box leads. [→ Page](/lore/the-sixth)
- **Star rating**—public 1–5 summary of a player's ability. The stars are a guide. The Ice is the authority.
- **Steam**—arena modifier at [The Dark Sauna](/teams/helsinki-howlers).
- **Syrup**—arena modifier at [The Oldest Rink](/teams/montreal-maples).

## T

- **T.A.P.E.**—Team Alignment & Patronage Evaluation. Le Council's psycho-athletic profiling instrument for team selection.
- **Thin Ice**—[match modifier](/modifiers/match#thin-ice). Skating reduced for everyone.
- **Tidal**—weird player modifier. Effects [Glacified](/lore/glacified). [→ Page](/modifiers/player#tidal)
- **Translated**—weird player modifier. Language [Glacified](/lore/glacified). [→ Page](/modifiers/player#translated)

## U

- **UITC**—Universal Ice Time Coordinated. Aligns with UTC by "non-coincidence." [→ Page](/guides/faq#what-does-uitc-stand-for-again)
- **Unexplained Ice Phenomenon**—Le Council's preferred term for anything it cannot explain. [→ Page](/lore/lpchuip)

## W

- **Wandering**—negative player modifier. [→ Page](/modifiers/player#wandering)
- **Whiteout**—arena modifier at [The Frozen Dock](/teams/busan-blizzards).
- **Wishes**—arena modifier at [The Sandy Parlor](/teams/dakar-djinns).
