---
title: Arena modifiers
description: Semi-permanent traits tied to team locations. One per arena, twenty in total.
---

Every team's home arena has a modifier. Unlike [match modifiers](/modifiers/match), arena
modifiers are always active at that venue, known in advance, and part of team identity.
Unlike [player modifiers](/modifiers/player), they don't travel—an arena modifier only
applies when the team is playing at home.

## Notable cases

- **Riviera** (Rimini) is the only **dual** arena modifier. One of two sub-effects—*Dolce Vita* or *Sunburn*—is revealed at game start. [Le Council](/lore/lpchuip) has failed to predict which will appear.
- **Isolation** (Vladivostok) suppresses *all* external modifiers on visiting players—including ones gained from [The Sixth](/lore/the-sixth). You arrive at The Terminal stripped bare.
- **Penguins** (McMurdo) is the only modifier whose enforcement mechanism is, officially, a group of spectators. The penguins are watching. They are disappointed in you.
- **Wishes** (Dakar) may reverse *any* outcome—a miss becomes a goal, a save becomes a miss. Be careful what you wish for.
