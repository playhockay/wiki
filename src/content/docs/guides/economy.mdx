## What Looneys are

Looneys are the official currency of Hockay. They are earned, spent, wagered, and
occasionally lost in circumstances [Le Council](/lore/lpchuip) declines to elaborate on.

- They **cannot** be exchanged for any sovereign currency.
- No sovereign currency can be exchanged for Looneys.
- [The Almighty Ice](/lore/almighty-ice) doesn't believe in fiat currencies.

## How to earn Looneys

- **Signup bonus**—on enrollment.
- **Daily login rewards**—keep coming back.
- **Correct match predictions**—payout based on odds.
- **Streak bonuses** for consecutive correct picks.
- **Community participation rewards**—voting, lore contributions.

## How to spend Looneys

- **Betting** on match outcomes (odds generated by [O.D.D.S.](/lore/organizations#odds)).
- More to come?

## How to lose Looneys

Betting incorrectly. Contributing to a team that then underperforms. Making decisions
Le Council cannot prevent you from making.

The economy is simple. The emotions are not.

## Odds

### O.D.D.S.—the official source

The **Office of Data-Driven Speculation** publishes every game's odds. Their methodology
is transparent, mathematical, and boring. They are proud of this.

Odds shift after every completed game as fresh results come in, so a team's odds on their
next scheduled game can tighten or lengthen throughout the evening.

They are sometimes wrong. **This is by design.** If the odds were always right, there
would be no upsets, and without upsets there would be no stories, and without stories
there would be no Hockay. Only Ice.

### O.M.E.N.—not affiliated

[O.M.E.N.](/lore/organizations#omen) publishes alternative forecasts based on "reasonating"
with The Almighty Ice. **NOT affiliated with LPCHUIP. NOT recognized. NOT endorsed.**
O.M.E.N. is sometimes wildly wrong. O.M.E.N. is sometimes unsettlingly accurate. Use at
your own risk.
