<Infobox variant="ice" title="One-line summary">
The Almighty Ice is the god of Hockay. Nobody started Hockay. The Almighty Ice chose.
</Infobox>

The Almighty Ice appeared one day in twenty places simultaneously, and where it appeared,
Hockay began. Nobody started the sport. The players were already there, already in jerseys,
already knowing the rules to a game that hadn't existed moments before.

The Almighty Ice is alive—or at least intent-ful. It is not a surface. It is not a
substance. It is present.

## The spreading

The Almighty Ice is spreading. Slowly, almost imperceptibly, but every season it's a
little more present. Unexplained Ice Phenomena are reported outside of arenas with
growing frequency. [Le Council](/lore/lpchuip) can neither confirm nor deny that this
trend is significant.

The macro-question the community should always be asking:

> *What happens when The Almighty Ice finishes spreading?*

## How it communicates

The Almighty Ice communicates through three channels, each more unsettling than the last:

1. **Through [Le Council](/lore/lpchuip)**—the preferred channel. Advisories are
   issued, conditions are named, games proceed.
2. **Directly to the ice**—arena conditions shift without notice.
   [Match modifiers](/modifiers/match) appear. The feed adapts.
3. **Silence**—the Ice goes quiet. No notifications. No observations.
   [Le Council finds the silence unusual but not unprecedented.](/modifiers/advisories)

## Appearances

- Every arena, continuously.
- As the playing surface in all 20 arenas.
- At the [Final](/guides/season-cycle#playoffs), which has one-hour periods. The
  Almighty Ice has more time there. Strange things happen.
