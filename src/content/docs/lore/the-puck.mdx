<Infobox variant="sixth" title="One-line summary">
The Puck is the Referee. The authority figure in Hockay is the object everyone fights over.
</Infobox>

Every goal is the Referee entering the net. Every shift is twelve skaters trying to
control the authority figure. The Puck is at every game simultaneously—the same entity,
or different shards of the same entity? Unknown.

## What The Puck does

- Officiates every match by being present on the ice.
- Decides, through whatever mechanism it uses, the outcome of every contested event.
- Speaks once per season. Exactly once.

## The annual sentence

After every [Final](/guides/season-cycle#playoffs), The Puck delivers a single sentence.
What it says when it speaks has, historically, been, well, historical. [Le Council](/lore/lpchuip)
advises paying attention when The Puck speaks. Le Council advises paying attention at all
other times as well.

The community maintains an archive of every sentence The Puck has ever spoken. New sentences
are parsed for weeks after they land.

## Star players it likes

The [Magnetic](/modifiers/player#magnetic) modifier says it outright: *"The Puck finds them."*
A Magnetic player is involved in more events than they should be. More shots, more hits,
more penalties. Great for a star. Terrible for a liability.

## Shards or singleton?

The Puck is simultaneously in use at all 10 concurrent matchday games. Either:

- There is one Puck, and it exists in ten places at once, or
- There are ten Pucks, and they are all the same Puck.

[Le Council](/lore/lpchuip) has investigated. [The investigation is Glacified](/lore/glacified).
