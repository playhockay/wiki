## Jean-Michel "JM" Laflèche

The play-by-play. Steady, professional, warm. Calls games like he's been doing it for forty
years, even though Hockay has only existed since [The Almighty Ice](/lore/almighty-ice)
appeared.

- **Bilingual.** Drops into French naturally for emphasis or excitement.
- Treats everything as completely normal.
- Has never once acknowledged that anything about Hockay is unusual.
- Signs off every broadcast the same way.
