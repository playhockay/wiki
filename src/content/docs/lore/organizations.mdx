[Le Permanent Council](/lore/lpchuip) is not the only acronym in Hockay. A handful of
related bodies exist, some confirmed, some partially [Glacified](/lore/glacified).

## Within LPCHUIP

### O.D.D.S.—Office of Data-Driven Speculation

An official department of [LPCHUIP](/lore/lpchuip). Staffed by actuaries, statisticians,
and one person who keeps insisting the numbers are "trying to tell us something" (they are
on probation).

O.D.D.S. publishes the official odds for every game. Their methodology is transparent,
mathematical, and boring. They are proud of this. **O.D.D.S. does not speculate.
O.D.D.S. calculates.**

The odds shift after every completed game as fresh results come in. They are sometimes
wrong. [This is intentional.](/guides/economy#odds)

### H.O.R.N.—Hockay Official Relay Network

The broadcast arm of Hockay. Scores, events, conditions, and advisories relayed in real
time. If something happens on the ice, H.O.R.N. announces it.

If H.O.R.N. announces something that did not happen on the ice, Le Council advises you to
act as though it did.

H.O.R.N. does not have opinions. H.O.R.N. does not speculate. H.O.R.N. relays.

## Affiliated institutions

### R.I.N.K.—The Royal Institute for Nascent Kinetics

A collaborating institution referenced in Le Council's public communications, including
the T.A.P.E. (Team Alignment & Patronage Evaluation) instrument. R.I.N.K. verifies results.
Le Council does not question R.I.N.K.'s methodology.

Academic, scientific, ceremonial—the exact nature of R.I.N.K. is [Glacified](/lore/glacified).

## Not recognized

### O.M.E.N.—Oracle for Mathematical Estimation and Numerology

**NOT affiliated with LPCHUIP. NOT recognized. NOT endorsed.**

O.M.E.N. is a rogue group that claims to have developed a method of "resonating" with
The Almighty Ice—or as they insist on calling it, "reasonating," a portmanteau they
invented and refuse to stop using. They believe the odds published by O.D.D.S. are
incomplete because they fail to account for The Ice's intentions.

O.M.E.N. publishes their own alternative forecasts, which are sometimes wildly wrong and
sometimes unsettlingly accurate.

> *Le Council's official position: Le Council is aware of the organization styling itself
> "O.M.E.N." Le Council does not recognize, endorse, or affiliate with this group.
> Le Council reminds all participants that The Almighty Ice does not communicate through
> spreadsheets, crystals, or whatever a "resonance array" is. Le Council advises participants
> to rely on O.D.D.S. for official forecasts and to disregard unauthorized numerology.*
