Le Permanent Council du Hockay and Unexplained Ice Phenomena is the governing body of the
sport. Half sports league, half paranormal investigation unit. They run the league but
they're also clearly trying to figure out what [The Almighty Ice](/lore/almighty-ice) is doing.

The bilingual name is never explained. The organization predates the language divide and
never bothered to pick a side.

## What Le Council does

- **Schedules matches** and publishes standings.
- **Issues [match advisories](/modifiers/advisories)** before every game.
- **Investigates [Unexplained Ice Phenomena](/lore/the-sixth)** with no discernible results.
- **Administers the [Glacification](/lore/glacified)** of information it cannot or will not share.
- **Classifies the identities of its own members** Glacified, out of "an abundance of caution for their personal safety."

## What Le Council does not do

- Control [The Almighty Ice](/lore/almighty-ice). Le Council observes. Sometimes it receives notifications.
- Endorse [O.M.E.N.](/lore/organizations#omen). Le Council is aware of the organization styling itself O.M.E.N. Le Council does not recognize, endorse, or affiliate with this group.
- Acknowledge conspiracies. Le Council does not entertain conspiracy theories, nor does it endorse the wearing of tin foil hats.

## Departments

- [**O.D.D.S.**](/lore/organizations#odds)—Office of Data-Driven Speculation. Publishes official betting odds.
- [**H.O.R.N.**](/lore/organizations#horn)—Hockay Official Relay Network. The broadcast arm.
- **Phonetics Bureau**—Maintains the official pronunciation guide. It's "Hockay" or "Hoqué." Not "Hockey."
