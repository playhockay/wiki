The Jambono resurfaces reality. It appears between periods and between seasons.
Caretaker or corruptor—nobody knows. The Jambono maintains [The Almighty Ice](/lore/almighty-ice),
but it's unclear whether it works for the Ice or is trying to contain it.

Sometimes when it resurfaces, things change. Stats shift. Goals retroactively didn't happen.
A player is different.

## What it does

### Between periods (in-game)

The Jambono appears between periods of every match to clean the ice. Most of the time this
is ordinary maintenance. Occasionally, during a game, a random event is retroactively
changed after it happens. The feed updates. The community notices. Le Council has no
official position on whether this constitutes time travel.

### Between seasons (The Resurfacing)

At the end of the season, The Jambono performs **The Resurfacing**—the preparation
of the league for what comes next. Player modifiers shift. Reality is adjusted. [JM](/lore/commentators)
previews the changes. The new season begins. Things may be different.

## Where it lives

[The Stone Opera](/teams/prague-phantoms) (Prague) has a parking spot for The Jambono in
the backstage corridor. This is the only arena that publicly acknowledges accommodating
The Jambono on a regular basis, though fans at other arenas report seeing the door to the
maintenance corridor open by itself between periods.
