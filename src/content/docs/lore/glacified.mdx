Glacified is the Hockay term for *classified*. Information that is Glacified is hidden —
either because [The Almighty Ice](/lore/almighty-ice) doesn't want it known, or because
the league has yet to reveal it.

Asking whether something is Glacified because it is structurally absent or because it is
being withheld is, itself, a Glacified question.

## Typical uses

- A player's stats after [returning from the Sixth](/lore/the-sixth) may be temporarily Glacified.
- **Modifier interactions** that haven't been mapped yet are Glacified.
- Six rooms in the basement of [The Oldest Rink](/teams/montreal-maples) are Glacified.
- [The identities of Le Council's members](/lore/lpchuip) are Glacified—not out of secrecy, but out of an abundance of caution for their personal safety.

## What Glacified is *not*

- **Secret.** Secrets can be discovered. Glacified information is structurally absent.
- **Hidden.** Hidden things have a location. Glacified things have a classification.
- **A cover-up.** Le Council does not cover anything up. Le Council declines to comment.

## Sample in-world usage

> "The effect of the Tidal modifier during the third period is Glacified."

> "Le Council has no comment. The conditions of tonight's match are Glacified."

> "Why there is a team in McMurdo: this information is Glacified."
